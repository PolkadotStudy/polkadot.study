# Polkadot Vault as a cold wallet for your chain.

## Time of completion

- This tutorial will take approximately 2 hours to complete.

## What you'll learn

- Host your own metadata portal
- Fetch chain specs and metadata
- Sign chain specs and metadata
- Add Polkadot Vault account to the PolkadotJs extension
- Sign transaction with your Polkadot Vault

## Prerequisites

- Basic knowledge about blockchain technology.
- knowledge about the ecosystem of Polkadot and Kusama.
