# substrate

> Substrate strives to be the best blockchain framework in which to build your
> custom blockchain. Substrate is not a blockchain in itself, but akin to a
> blockchain SDK framework. Substrate empowers you to build beyond the
> capabilities of others, giving you the freedom to define your chain however
> you want. (Gavin Wood)
> [source](https://substrate.io/vision/substrate-and-polkadot/))
